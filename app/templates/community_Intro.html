About Community

Here, you can find like-minded people to play basketball with. It is not only a way to find friends who play basketball with you nearby, but also a new way to start a basketball life and make friends in an unfamiliar city.

We are dedicated to helping people who want to play basketball and want to find someone to play basketball with.

  • Our focus is to make it easier for you to enter the basketball community
  • We are not just a basketball community platform, we hope that you can find your good friends through basketball
  • I hope you can explore more than just basketball stories here.
  • We care more about your mental health in this community than your basketball skills.
  • We hope that people from all walks of life can come together and share their stories with basketball here.
